### **Part 2: The Core Workflow (0:30 - 1:30)**
**Visual**: Log in and go to the Dashboard. Create a link.
**Speaker**:

Once created, it appears instantly on my dashboard. Notice the 'Custom' badge? That’s for branded links that stand out."

### **Tips for a Winning Video:**
1. **Screen Recording**: Use OBS or Loom.
2. **Audio**: Use a clear microphone; quiet background.
3. **Cursor**: Use a cursor highlighter or make it larger for better visibility.
4. **Enthusiasm**: Speak with energy—you're proud of what you've built!
